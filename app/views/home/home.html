Prelude

Math is everywhere.
From the smallest Planck length, denoted lP, which is equal to 1.616199(97)x10−35 meters to the biggest, observable universe, which is 28.5 Gparsecs (93 billion light-years, 8.8×1026 metres or 5.5×1023 miles).

We can easily find Fibonacci or imaginary numbers around us in everyday life.

This site will try to explain or describe mathematics the most easiest ways possible: pictures, drawings, videos, or simple formulas.
